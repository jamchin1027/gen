Loop Equations Used By This Tool
Phase margin is computed from the selected stage open-loop transfer H(jw) at |H(jwcp)|=1: PM = 180deg + angle(H(jwcp)). Closed-loop bandwidth is the -3 dB frequency of T(s)=H(s)/(1+H(s)).
Fast-lock estimate assumes lock_flag=1 when ref/fb rising-edge error is below threshold, then requires hold_count feedback-clock cycles: T_hold=hold_count/Ffb, T_settle≈settling_factor/(2*pi*BWcl).
Fast Lock
H(s)=H1(s)
H1=(ICP*Kpd*KVCO*(R1*C1*s+1))/(N*s^2*(R1*C1*C2*s+C1+C2))
Fine Lock
H(s)=H1(s)+H2(s)
H1=(ICP*K1*KVCO*(R1*C1*s+1))/(N*s^2*(R1*C1*C2*s+C1+C2))
H2=(ICP*K2*KVCO*(R1*C1*s+1))/(N*s^2*(R1*C1*C2*R3*C3*s^2+(R1*C1*C2+(C1+C2)*R3*C3)*s+(C1+C2)))
K1=3/19, K2=16/19
CDR
fine_lock=1 switches detector from PFD to BBPD.
Kbbpd=TD*2/(sqrt(2*pi)*jitter_rms_UI)
ICP_static=(|IUP|+|IDN|)/2
ICP_eff=(ICP_static/(2*pi))*(pulse_width_UI/update_interval_UI)*BBPD_scale*Kbbpd
H(s)=H1(s)+H2(s), N=1
H1=(ICP_eff*K1*KVCO*(R1*C1*s+1))/(s^2*(R1*C1*C2*s+C1+C2))
H2=(ICP_eff*K2*KVCO*(R1*C1*s+1))/(s^2*(R1*C1*C2*R3*C3*s^2+(R1*C1*C2+(C1+C2)*R3*C3)*s+(C1+C2)))
